Adoption Information

At Paws & Whiskers Adoption Center, we've created a warm, welcoming space where homeless cats and dogs can find their second chance at happiness. Our carefully curated adoption program focuses on making perfect matches between pets and people, ensuring every adoption is successful and long-lasting. We take pride in our thorough vetting process - all our animals receive comprehensive medical care including vaccinations, spay/neuter surgeries, microchipping, and thorough health screenings before they're ready for their new homes.


What sets us apart is our personalized approach to adoption; we take time to understand each animal's unique personality and needs, as well as each adopter's lifestyle and expectations. Our facility features comfortable meet-and-greet rooms where potential adopters can interact with pets in a stress-free environment, and our knowledgeable staff is always available to offer guidance and answer questions. Beyond adoption day, we provide ongoing support through training resources, post-adoption check-ins, and access to our network of pet care professionals. We also operate a small boutique stocked with high-quality supplies to help new pet parents get started, offering exclusive discounts to our adopters. At Paws & Whiskers, we don't just place animals in homes - we build lasting relationships and create happy endings, one tail wag and purr at a time. Visit us today and discover how the right pet can transform your life while you're transforming theirs.
