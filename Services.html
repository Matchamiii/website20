Services


"Complete Pet Care Services: Wellness, Grooming & Emergency Support at Paws & Whiskers"

At Paws & Whiskers, we're proud to offer a complete range of professional pet care services designed to keep your furry family members healthy, happy, and thriving at every stage of their lives. Our full-service veterinary clinic provides comprehensive medical care including wellness exams, vaccinations, and advanced diagnostics like digital x-rays - all performed by our compassionate team of animal health professionals who take the time to understand your pet's unique needs. For your pet's comfort and hygiene, our expert grooming salon offers everything from basic nail trims to full-service spa packages featuring breed-specific haircuts, soothing baths with premium products, and specialized treatments for skin conditions. We know emergencies don't wait for business hours, which is why we provide urgent care services with state-of-the-art equipment to handle critical situations when every second counts. For senior pets or those facing serious health challenges, our gentle end-of-life care services provide comfort and dignity, including in-home euthanasia options and beautiful memorial keepsakes to honor your companion's memory. What truly sets us apart is our holistic approach - we don't just treat symptoms, but consider your pet's overall wellbeing, offering nutritional counseling, behavioral advice, and preventive care plans tailored to your pet's age, breed and lifestyle. From that first playful puppy or kitten visit through their golden years, we're here to be your trusted partner in pet care, combining cutting-edge medicine with old-fashioned compassion in our warm, welcoming clinic designed to make every visit as stress-free as possible for both pets and their people.
